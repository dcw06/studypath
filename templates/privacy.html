Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in using a third-party provider (Google, Facebook, GitHub), we receive:

  • Your email address
  • Your display name (if provided)
  • A unique identifier from the provider

We do not receive or store your password from any third-party provider.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To identify you when you sign in
  • To display your name within the app

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on the device running this application. We do not transmit your data to external servers beyond what is required for OAuth authentication.

4. Facebook Data

If you sign in with Facebook, we receive only your email and name via the Facebook Login API. We do not access your Facebook friends, posts, or any other data. You can revoke access at any time from your Facebook App Settings.

5. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data at any time. To do so:

  • Use the in-app account deletion feature, or
  • Submit a deletion request via our data deletion page
  • For Facebook users: visit Facebook App Settings, find this app, and remove it — we will delete your data within 30 days

6. Cookies & Sessions

We use a session cookie solely to keep you signed in during your session. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or deletion requests, contact us at: privacy@yourapp.com
